Members Online • holytriplem Top 1% Commenter What lasting effects did hosting the Olympics have on your city, or even your country as a whole? Misc For the sake of having a wide range of answers I guess we can include both the Summer and Winter Olympics. The London 2012 Olympics placed a great emphasis on leaving a lasting legacy and avoiding the mistakes that Athens made in 2004. A large post-industrial area contaminated with toxic waste was converted into a park, and a use has been found for most of the old venues. However, it failed in a lot of its long-term goals, namely encouraging greater participation in sport, and providing affordable housing. Nonetheless, it's generally remembered quite fondly (probably in part because it took place during the two weeks of nice weather in an otherwise incredibly appalling summer...) The previous London Olympics took place in 1948 - when the country was still in the process of recovering from war.
