Djokovic won his record-setting 23rd Grand Slam singles title, breaking a tie with Rafael Nadal for the most by a man. It's also his third championship on the red clay of Roland Garros, making him the first man with at least three singles titles at each of the four major tournaments. PARIS — Novak Djokovic made clear for years this was his goal. What drove him. What inspired him.
