Grand Slam tournaments [ edit ] Djokovic has won an all-time record 24 Grand Slam titles and he is the only man to achieve a triple Career Grand Slam by winning each of the four majors at least three times. Djokovic is also the only man in tennis history to be the reigning champion of all four majors at once across three different surfaces. He is the record holder for the most Grand Slam finals played (37) and the only player to reach at least 7 finals at each of the four majors.
