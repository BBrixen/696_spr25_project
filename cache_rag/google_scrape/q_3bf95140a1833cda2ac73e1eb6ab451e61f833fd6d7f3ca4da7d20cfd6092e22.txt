Find sources: "Tanya Allen" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( June 2019 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Tanya Allen Born 1975 (age 49–50) Toronto , Ontario , Canada Occupation Actress Years active 1993–present Awards Gemini Award Tanya Allen (born 1975) is a Canadian actress , best known as the harried assistant Audrey on the CBC sitcom The Newsroom . At age 15, she began training at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in New York City . In addition to her film and television work, Allen has also performed on stage with Second City in Los Angeles .
