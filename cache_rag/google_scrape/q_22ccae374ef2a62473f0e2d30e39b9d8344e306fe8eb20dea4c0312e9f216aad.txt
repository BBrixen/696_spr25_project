What: Tesla First Quarter 2021 Financial Results Q&A Webcast When: Monday, April 26, 2021 Time: 2:30 p.m. Pacific Time / 5:30 p.m. Eastern Time Q1 2021 Update: https://ir.tesla.com Webcast: https://ir.tesla.com (live and replay) The webcast will be archived on the company’s website following the call.
