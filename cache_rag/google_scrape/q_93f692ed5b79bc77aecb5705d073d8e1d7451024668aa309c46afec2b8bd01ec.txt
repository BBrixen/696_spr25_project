Mandatory Credit: Ron Chenoy-Imagn Images College Football List of Walter Camp Award Winners December 14, 2024 | 12:15 PM EST Named after the “Father of American Football,” Walter Camp Award winners are honored for being the College Football Player of the Year. Awarded since 1967 by the Walter Camp Football Foundation, winners are decided by a group of FBS head coaches and sports information directors. Walter Camp Award winners are listed by year below.
