Mike Bryan and Jack Sock won the title, defeating Raven Klaasen and Michael Venus in the final, 6–3, 6–7 (7–9) , 6–3, 5–7, 7–5. [ 1 ] It was Bryan's 17th Grand Slam men's doubles title (a new Open Era record ), and his first with a partner other than his brother Bob . Bryan regained the ATP No. 1 doubles ranking at the end of the tournament, becoming the oldest no. 1 player in the history of the ATP rankings .
