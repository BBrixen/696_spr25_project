Career [ edit ] Richards' first feature film was Chloé Zhao 's Songs My Brothers Taught Me . [ 1 ] For his work, he was nominated for the Independent Spirit Award for Best Cinematography . [ 2 ] The duo would later reteam to do The Rider and Nomadland . For the latter, Richards received numerous accolades and awards, including a BAFTA Award , an Independent Spirit Award , and a nomination for an Academy Award .
