[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] Ehelepola has recently starred as Dennis in the ABC TV series, The Newsreader , [ 5 ] [ 1 ] and is also cast as Jagadish in the streaming series Nautilus . [ 6 ] [ 1 ] Early life [ edit ] Ehelepola was born in New Zealand [ 7 ] [ 4 ] [ 1 ] and raised in both Sri Lanka [ 7 ] [ 4 ] [ 1 ] and Australia . [ 7 ] [ 4 ] [ 1 ] Ehelepola attended Hampton Senior High School in the Perth suburb of Morley , Western Australia . Career [ edit ] Ehelepola has also starred in a number of Australian TV series, including No Activity , [ 8 ] [ 9 ] Stateless , [ 8 ] The Straits , [ 7 ] and as Lionel in the Australian comedy series Preppers . [ 10 ] Due to greater opportunities for actors from diverse backgrounds, Ehelepola relocated [ 1 ] to Los Angeles and appeared in a number of US television shows whilst residing there, including the AMC series Lodge 49 [ 8 ] and the Netflix series Lady Dynamite . [ 8 ] He was also cast in the role of Ramtish in the Disney live action film, Mulan . Ehelepola followed his passion of acting, taking the path of washing dishes, serving coffee and acting classes on the way to building a successful acting career. [ 3 ] After residing in Los Angeles for a period of time, Ehelepola returned with his family to Australia and currently resides in NSW .
