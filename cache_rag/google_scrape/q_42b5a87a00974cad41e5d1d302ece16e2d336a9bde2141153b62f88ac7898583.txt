The TV videogame adaptation has added many new faces and, in some cases, new characters , to the series’ second installment. Following the events of the Season 1 finale, titled “Look For the Light,” Joel ( Pedro Pascal ) and Ellie ( Bella Ramsey ) have settled in Jackson, Wyoming, hoping to move on after an attempted operation on Ellie for her medicinal blood. Little do they know, their collective pasts are catching up to them. Related Stories News ‘The Last Of Us’ Season 2: Everything We Know So Far News ‘The Last Of Us’ Season 2 Cast: Who’s Who? Find all the details regarding The Last Of Us Season 2 episode release schedule below: Watch on Deadline When does The Last of Us Season 2 premiere? The Last of Us Season 2 debuts Sunday, April 13 at 9 p.m. ET/PT. How many episodes are in Season 2 of The Last of Us ? Season 2 will unfold in 7 episodes. Are new episodes of The Last Of Us Season 2 releasing weekly or all at once? One episode will launch per week on Sunday nights. RELATED: ‘The Last Of Us’ Season 2: Everything We Know So Far The full rollout for The Last of Us Season 2 episode release schedule can be found below: Episode 1 – “Future Days”: Sunday, April 13 Episode 2: Sunday, April 20 (Easter) Episode 3: Sunday, April 27 Episode 4: Sunday, May 4 Episode 5: Sunday, May 11 Episode 6: Sunday, May 18 Episode 7: Sunday, May 25 (FINALE) Who is in The Last of Us Season 2? Pedro Pascal and Bella Ramsey return as Joel Miller and Ellie, respectively. Gabriel Luna also returns as Joel’s younger brother Tommy. New characters this season include Isabela Merced’s Dina, Kaitlyn Dever’s Abby, Young Mazino’s Jesse, Tati Gabrielle’s Nora and Jeffrey Wright’s Isaac.Ariela Barer will portray Mel, and Danny Ramirez will play Manny. Catherine O’Hara will also appear in Season 2. RELATED: ‘The Last Of Us’ Season 2 New Cast: Who Is Joining The Next Installment? It doesn’t look like Bill (Nick Offerman) or Frank (Murray Bartlett) will return either after their breakout episode in Season 1 . Executive Producer Craig Mazin ruled out a return of the characters. What is The Last of Us Season 2 about? The logline for Season 2 reads, “Five years after the events of the first season, Joel and Ellie are drawn into conflict with each other and a world even more dangerous and unpredictable than the one they left behind.” What happened in Season 1 of The Last of Us ? Season 1 of HBO’s videogame adaptation watched Joel and Ellie unite on a collision course to find a cure for cordyceps. Through their harrowing travels stopping in cities like Kansas City, Salt Like City and more, the pair fought off their fair share of Infected people, rebellious factions and those struggling for power. In the finale, though, when Joel realized how harvesting the cure would affect Ellie — cordyceps grows in the brain — he killed many Fireflies to get to her before the operation and take her back to Jackson, Wyoming.
