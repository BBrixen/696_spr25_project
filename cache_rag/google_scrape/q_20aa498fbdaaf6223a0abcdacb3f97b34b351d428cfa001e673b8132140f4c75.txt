Last year's winner Bong Joon-Ho presents the Oscar for Best Directing to Chloe Zhao at the 93rd Oscars in Los Angeles [Todd Wawrychuk/A.M.P.A.S./Handout via Reuters] Published On 26 Apr 2021 26 Apr 2021 The Academy Awards, or Oscars, have been handed out at a ceremony in Los Angeles and were broadcast live on ABC television. The 93rd annual ceremony, held at Union Station on Sunday, was stripped back to its bare essentials as a result of the coronavirus pandemic.
