[ 3 ] Name [ edit ] The name Klanec is shared with several other settlements in Slovenia. The name is derived from the Slovene common noun klanec , which may variously mean 'steep path upwards', 'path through a narrow area', and 'isolated, tucked-away place'. [ 4 ] In the past the German name was Klanz . [ 2 ] In the 18th century, the northern part of the village was known as Dragomenja vas .
