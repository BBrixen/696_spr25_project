The Division I Men's Basketball Oversight Committee announced Aug. 3 as the deadline for student-athletes to remove their name from the NBA draft. Per ESPN's Adrian Wojnarowski , the NBA will hold this year's draft on Oct. 15. The NBA said on May 1 that the draft lottery (May 19) and draft combine (May 19-24) had been postponed because of the coronavirus pandemic. After that announcement, the NCAA indefinitely extended the deadline for student-athletes to withdraw from the draft until the NBA made a final determination. A makeup date for the scouting combine has yet to be announced, but Wojnarowski reported the lottery will take place on Aug. 25. In a normal year, student-athletes would have 10 days after the combine to withdraw from the draft if they wanted to return maintain college eligibility.
