E3 2019: Keanu Reeves Reveals Cyberpunk 2077 Release Date During Microsoft's Xbox Conference Coming in 2020, with Keanu Reeves in tow. By Eddie Makuch on June 10, 2019 at 11:33PM PDT 8 Comments Where to buy Cyberpunk 2077 There are currently no available deals GameSpot may get a commission from retail offers. Cyberpunk 2077 , the ambitious new first-person sci-fi RPG from The Witcher III developer CD Projekt Red, finally has a release date. During Microsoft's Xbox E3 press conference , we learned that the game will launch during the first half of 2020, on April 16. The game is releasing on Xbox One, PS4, and PC. The news came following the reveal that none other than John wick actor Keanu Reeves will have a role in Cyberpunk 2077 . Reeves himself appeared on Microsoft's stage and was the one to announce Cyberpunk 2077's release date, which is really pretty perfect.
