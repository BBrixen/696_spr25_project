[ 1 ] [ 2 ] It uses OpenAI's advanced models to expand practical automation capabilities for users in daily activities. [ 3 ] Operator was launched on 23 January 2025. [ 4 ] It was released as a limited-access research preview to Pro-tier subscribers in the United States on February 1, 2025, with future plans to broaden availability. [ 5 ] Performance and limitations [ edit ] In benchmark assessments, Operator achieved notable success, scoring 38.1% on OSWorld benchmarks (OS-level tasks) and 58.1% on WebArena benchmarks (web interactions). [ 6 ] However, it has not yet reached human-level accuracy and faces limitations with intricate user interfaces and extended workflows. [ 3 ] Safety and privacy [ edit ] OpenAI emphasizes privacy and safety measures within Operator, including stringent data protection protocols and built-in safety checks designed to prevent unauthorized sensitive actions or information misuse. [ 3 ] Availability [ edit ] Initially, Operator is only available to ChatGPT Pro subscribers in the U.S., with plans for broader availability to Plus, Team, and Enterprise users in the future.
