In the top categories, Guillermo del Toro claimed the honor for best director for The Shape of Water , while Gary Oldman nabbed best actor for Darkest Hour and Frances McDormand won for best actress for Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri. Meanwhile, Sam Rockwell took home an early best supporting actor win for Three Billboards while Allison Janney claimed a win for I, Tonya in the supporting actress category. For screenwriting, James Ivory claimed a win for Call Me by Your Name in the adapted screenplay category while Jordan Peele won for Get Out in the original screenplay category.
