Infinity War ( deleted scene ) Avengers: Endgame Character Doctor Fine Theo Broussard Bert Bobby Thanos Joseph Vincent "Joe" Russo co-directed Captain America: The Winter Soldier , Captain America: Civil War , Avengers: Infinity War , Avengers: Endgame , Avengers: Doomsday , and Avengers: Secret Wars with his brother Anthony Russo . He also directed the Agent Carter episode Bridge and Tunnel . He portrayed Doctor Fine and the man interviewing Peggy Carter in Captain America: The Winter Soldier , Theo Broussard in Captain America: Civil War , Bert in a deleted scene of Avengers: Infinity War [ 2 ] and Bobby in Avengers: Endgame . He also performed the motion capture for Thanos in the scenes from Avengers: Endgame where Thanos interacted with Nebula due to Josh Brolin 's unavailability when those scenes were shot. [ 3 ] He also co-directed the post-credits scene of Ant-Man , which was a scene taken from Captain America: Civil War , and the mid-credits scene of Captain Marvel with Anthony. [ 4 ] He was an executive producer on Avengers: Doomsday and Avengers: Secret Wars . [ 5 ] He was interviewed for the documentary Marvel Studios: Assembling a Universe . Contents 1 Awards 2 Trivia 3 Gallery 4 References 5 External Links Awards [ ] Together with Anthony Russo , he was nominated for a Saturn Award for Best Director for his work on Captain America: The Winter Soldier . Together with Anthony Russo , he was nominated for a NAACP Image Award for Best Director for his work on Captain America: Civil War . Together with Anthony Russo , he was nominated for a Saturn Award for Best Director for his work on Captain America: Civil War . Trivia [ ] Joe and Anthony Russo were the first directors hired to direct three MCU films, and the only ones to direct four, five and six MCU films. Joe Russo is one of the actors who has portrayed multiple characters in the Marvel Cinematic Universe . Joe Russo is the father of Sophia Russo , Basil Russo , Ava Russo and Lia Russo , the brother of Angela Russo-Otstot , the brother-in-law of Ann Russo and the uncle of Julian Russo , Dante Rosalina and Agostino Rosalina . Joe Russo, and his brother Anthony, previously worked with Danny Pudi , Jim Rash , Ken Jeong , Yvette Nicole Brown , Aaron Himelstein and Brie Larson in Community . Joe Russo, and his brother Anthony, previously worked with Michael Douglas in You, Me and Dupree . Joe Russo, and his brother Anthony, worked again with Chadwick Boseman in 21 Bridges . Joe Russo, and his brother Anthony, worked again with Chris Hemsworth and Sam Hargrave in Extraction and Extraction 2 . Joe Russo, and his brother Anthony, worked again with Kevin Feige and Cobie Smulders on The Simpsons . Joe Russo, and his brother Anthony, worked again with Tom Holland in Cherry . Joe Russo, and his brother Anthony, worked again with Aaron Lazar in Everything Everywhere All at Once . Joe Russo, and his brother Anthony, worked again with Christopher Markus , Stephen McFeely , Chris Evans , Alfre Woodard and Callan Mulvey in The Gray Man . Joe Russo, and his brother Anthony, worked again with Chris Pratt , Anthony Mackie , Alan Silvestri and Jeffrey Ford in The Electric State . Joe Russo shares his birthday with Vin Diesel and Elsa Pataky .
