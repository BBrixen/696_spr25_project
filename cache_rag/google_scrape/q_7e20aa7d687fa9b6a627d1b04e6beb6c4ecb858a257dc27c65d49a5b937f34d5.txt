Discussion I wasn’t necessarily thinking about this occasion, until I happened upon a tweet that reminded me Animal Crossing: New Horizons released on March 20, 2020. Though I don’t play the game nearly as often as I did back then, I find myself weirdly nostalgic for that time and the experience of sharing such pure escapism with so many others. I’m honestly not sure what other gaming phenomenon compares to that. It felt extremely intimate at a time when the world was incredibly isolated. Animal Crossing with always give me rich, warm feelings because of that. What does the game mean to you?
