Members Online • AweYissBoi Mario Odyssey's two player is actually extremely fun and is quite possibly the best 2P experince i’ve has in a 1P game (spoiler free) Discussion false Let me start of by saying; I love Odyssey, everything about it. Even it’s 2P mode. But here’s the thing; it’s not bad at all. Cappy has complete control over the camera, can jump, ground pound, throw himself, and can go wherever he pleases while Mario is in the state of a captured enemy. This being said Mario can also throw cappy and use commands on Cappy like in 1P mode, so this mode is perfectly friendly to younger kids with less gaming experince. The only downside there is is that Cappy can not participate in some minor side activites, but aside from that everything goes. I feel people are confusing this to Galaxy 2’s (and possibly 1’s, never played the first so i wouldn’t know) multiplayer, which was horrendous in my opinion. Essentially, P1 was Mario and P2 was a Luma that could not commit any other action beside shooting star bits to stun enemy’s, not even moving. TL;DR: Mario’s 2P mode is really good (and not at all similar to Galaxy 2’s (1’s?)) and Cappy is pretty much a (slightly) less complex Mario with a few alterations Read more Archived post.
