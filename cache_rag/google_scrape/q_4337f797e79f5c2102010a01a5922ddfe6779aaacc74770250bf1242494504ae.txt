The first season was released on 4 November 2016, [ 1 ] the second on 8 December 2017, [ 2 ] the third on 17 November 2019, [ 3 ] the fourth on 15 November 2020, [ 4 ] the fifth on 9 November 2022, [ 5 ] and the sixth between 16 November and 14 December 2023. [ 6 ] During the course of the series, 60 episodes of The Crown were released over six seasons, between November 4, 2016, and December 14, 2023.
