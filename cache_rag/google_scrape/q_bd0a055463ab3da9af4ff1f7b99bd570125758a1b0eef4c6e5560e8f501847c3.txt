It was her first major title. Osaka became the first Japanese player to win a major singles title. [ 1 ] [ 2 ] With the win, she made her top-10 rankings debut. Williams was attempting to equal Margaret Court 's all-time record of 24 major singles titles, and to set a new Open Era record by winning a seventh US Open singles title. Sloane Stephens was the defending champion, but lost to Anastasija Sevastova in the quarterfinals. Simona Halep 's first-round loss to Kaia Kanepi marked the first time in the Open Era that the top seed lost in the first round. [ 3 ] The losses of Halep in the first round and second seed Caroline Wozniacki in the second round marked the first time that the top two seeds failed to reach the third round, and the second time this happened at any major in the Open Era (following the 2014 French Open ).
