Sixteen different award watch lists were announced over a 10-day span in July, starting with the Bednarik and Maxwell Awards on July 10 and ending with the Walter Camp Award on July 21. Watch lists will be shortened throughout the season and this year's winners will be announced on Dec. 7 at the annual Home Depot College Football Awards, hosted by the College Football Hall of Fame in Atlanta, Georgia.
