Agencies NBC 's hit crime thriller, The Blacklist , has been keeping viewers captivated throughout its tenth and final season, which premiered in February 2023. As fans eagerly await the conclusion of the series, many wonder how many episodes are left in this thrilling season. The tenth season features the talented James Spader as the enigmatic Raymond "Red" Reddington, alongside Diego Klattenhoff as Donald Ressler, Hisham Tawfiq as Dembe Zuma, Anya Banerjee as Siya Malik , and Harry Lennix as Harold Cooper. Although there is still some time before bidding farewell to this popular show, the end is drawing near. So, how many episodes are left in The Blacklist Season 10? Let's find out! The Blacklist Season 10 comprises a total of 22 episodes, which is the standard episode count for network shows. As of June 23, 2023, 18 episodes have already aired. Notably, previous seasons also followed the 22-episode format, except for Seasons 3 and 7, which had 23 and 19 episodes respectively. Here are the remaining episode titles: Episode 19 — "Room 417" Live Events Episode 20 — "Arthur Hudson" Episode 21 — " Raymond Reddington : Pt. 1" Episode 22 — "Raymond Reddington: Good Night" If you're wondering when the upcoming episodes of The Blacklist Season 10 will be released, mark your calendars for Thursday nights at 8 p.m. ET on NBC. Additionally, you can catch up on episodes the following day via streaming on Peacock. However, keep in mind that the episodes are only available for a limited time. Currently, the first nine seasons of the series are streaming on Netflix, and Season 10 is expected to join the platform soon after the season and series finale airs. Here is the release schedule for the remaining episodes: Episode 19 — June 29 Episode 20 — July 6 Episode 21 — July 13 Episode 22 — July 13 The Blacklist Season 10 finale, which also serves as the series finale, is set to air on Thursday, July 13, 2023, starting at 8 p.m. ET and concluding at 10 p.m. ET on NBC. This special two-hour block will feature back-to-back episodes, "Raymond Reddington: Pt. 1" and "Raymond Reddington: Good Night." FAQs Q1:How many episodes are present in Season 10 of The Blacklist? There are a total of 22 episodes in The Blacklist Season 10. The Blacklist Season 10 finale is set to air on Thursday, July 13, 2023. Q2:Where can you watch The Blacklist Season 10? You can watch The Blacklist Season 10 on NBC on Thursdays at 8 p.m. ET. On the following day, it can be watched on Peacock.
