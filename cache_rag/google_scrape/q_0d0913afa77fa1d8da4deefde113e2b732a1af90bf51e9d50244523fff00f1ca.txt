Kneafsey started her career as a child actress, featuring in such roles as June, the youngest child of historical figure George Mottershead , in the BBC1 drama Our Zoo (2014), [ 1 ] [ 2 ] and the young character Christine who works in, and later burns down, the eponymous The Bookshop (2017). She has made numerous television, film and West End stage appearances.
