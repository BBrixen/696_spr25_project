Get started Frequently asked questions Here are some frequently asked questions (and answers!) about Nintendo Switch Online and Nintendo Switch Online + Expansion Pack. How long does Nintendo Switch Online take to activate? You can begin enjoying your benefits immediately when you purchase your membership through Nintendo eShop on your device, upon redeeming a valid download code purchased through select retailers, or when added to an active Nintendo Switch Online Family Membership. How to use voice chat in the Nintendo Switch Online App? From your Nintendo Switch system, start the compatible software using the same Nintendo Account as used on the Nintendo Switch Online app. Then start the game mode that supports voice chat. On the smart device, select "Start" after receiving the prompt asking, "Start voice chat?" As you play online, other players using the Nintendo Switch Online app can join the voice chat lobby as they join your online game. Can you access Nintendo Switch Online without a console? The Nintendo Music app and Nintendo Switch Online smartphone app can be accessed without a Nintendo Switch system. All other benefits, including the classic games libraries, require a Nintendo Switch system. How can I add family members to Nintendo Switch Online? Only the admin Nintendo Account for the family group can add additional Nintendo Accounts. Please see this page for more information: https://en-americas-support.nintendo.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/22324 Can I gift a Nintendo Switch Online membership to someone? Download codes for Individual and Family Memberships (including Nintendo Switch Online + Expansion Pack memberships) can be purchased through select retailers.
