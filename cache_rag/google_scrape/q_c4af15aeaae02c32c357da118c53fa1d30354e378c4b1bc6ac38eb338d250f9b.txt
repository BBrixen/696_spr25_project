Winners of Wimbledon Men's Tennis Tournament Home > Events > Tennis > Grand Slam > Wimbledon > Winners > Men Wimbledon Winners - Men's Singles Here is the winners list of the Wimbledon men's singles event from each year it has been held. The player with the most titles is Roger Federer with eight (2003–2007, 2009, 2012, 2017). Djokovic's win in 2022 was his seventh (and his 21st major singles title overall). Pete Sampras also won Wimbledon 7 times. See also the Women's singles winners list.
