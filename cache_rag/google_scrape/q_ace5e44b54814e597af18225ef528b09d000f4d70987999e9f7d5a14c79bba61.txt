After debuting in 2023, viewers were immediately sucked into the video game-inspired story following Joel ( Pedro Pascal ), a man tasked with protecting young Ellie ( Bella Ramsey ) who could hold the key to humanity’s survival in a world plagued by a fungal virus. Below, we’re breaking down everything we know about Season 2, from a premiere date and casting to how many episodes are anticipated to air. When Will The Last of Us Season 2 Premiere? HBO The video game-inspired drama will officially return on Sunday, April 13th at 9/8c on HBO and Max. How Many Episodes Will The Last of Us Season 2 Have? Despite Season 1 featuring nine episodes, Season 2 will only include seven installments this time around. Confirmed by HBO, the episode count was unveiled by co-creator, writer, and executive producer Neil Druckmann at the Consumer Electronics Show. Is The Last of Us Season 2 Filming? The Last of Us has officially wrapped filming on Season 2 as of August 2024, as editor Timothy Good teased on social media. “Wrapped! Congratulations to the incredible production crew of #TheLastOfUs season 2. We will bring your hard work home now! #editing #postproduction #TLOU @HBO @StreamOnMax,” Good wrote alongside photos posted to X (formerly known as Twitter). Does The Last of Us Season 2 Have a Trailer? ﻿ No full trailer has been unveiled quite yet, but HBO did unveil a lengthy two-minute teaser offering a closer look at the newest characters joining the mix. The teaser was released in celebration of The Last of Us Day on September 26th. See the thrilling teaser, above. Since then, another new teaser has been released, giving viewers a closer look at the newest characters joining the series, see it below: ﻿ Who Stars in The Last of Us Season 2? As previously reported, Pascal and Ramsey will be back as Joel and Ellie alongside an ensemble that includes Gabriel Luna , Rutina Wesley Kaitlyn Dever , Isabela Merced , Young Mazino , Ariela Barer , Tati Gabrielle , Spencer Lord , Danny Ramirez , Catherine O’Hara , and Jeffrey Wright . For more info on the newest characters joining the series, check out our cast guide here . Meanwhile, it has since been revealed by Variety that Joe Pantoliano , Alanna Ubach , Ben Ahlers , Hettienne Park , Robert John Burke , and Noah Lamanna will appear in supporting roles. What Is The Last of Us Season 2 About? Season 2 will continue the story started in Season 1 with Joel and Ellie carrying on after he whisked her away from the Fireflies at the end of Season 1. Likely taking direct inspiration from the second installment of The Last of Us video game franchise, they’ll encounter new characters and face new challenges in the brutal post-apocalyptic world. As the Season 2 logline teases, After five years of peace following the events of the first season, Joel and Ellie’s collective past catches up to them, drawing them into conflict with each other and a world even more dangerous and unpredictable than the one they left behind. Does The Last of Us Season 2 Have Posters? HBO HBO unveiled several art posters for Season 2 featuring Ramsey and Pascal as well as new characters joining the fray. See it, above. The Last of Us is also executive produced by Carolyn Strauss, Jacqueline Lesko, Cecil O’Connor, Asad Qizilbash, Carter Swan, and Evan Wells.
