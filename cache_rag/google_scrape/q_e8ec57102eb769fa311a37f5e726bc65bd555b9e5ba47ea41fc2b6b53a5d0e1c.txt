By: KTNV Staff Posted and last updated LAS VEGAS (KTNV) — The Las Vegas Raiders are honoring this year's National Teacher Of The Year -- Las Vegas' own Juliana Urtubey. PREVIOUS STORY: Las Vegas elementary school teacher named National Teacher of the Year Members of the silver and black, including the Raiderettes, visited Booker Elementary School. They surprised Ms. Urtubey with a Raiders-themed gift basket. Ms. Urtubey teaches special education. She is also Nevada's first Latina teacher of the year since 1992.
