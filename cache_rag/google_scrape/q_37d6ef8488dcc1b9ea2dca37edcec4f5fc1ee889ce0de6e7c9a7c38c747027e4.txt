Reeves introduced the Cyberpunk 2077 date reveal trailer during Microsoft's 2019 E3 conference, where it was also revealed that his likeness will be included in the game as the character Johnny Silverhand . He's also the co-owner of the real-life ARCH company which makes custom motorcycles that are featured in the game. Contents 1 About 2 Trivia 3 Gallery 4 Videos 5 External Links 6 References About [ ] Keanu Charles Reeves, whose first name means "cool breeze over the mountains" in Hawaiian, was born September 2, 1964 in Beirut, Lebanon. Some of his more well known roles include Neo in The Matrix series and as the eponymous John Wick in the John Wick series. Prior to Cyberpunk 2077 , he only did voice work for one other video game, Enter the Matrix , in 2003. On September 6, 2022, it was announced by Keanu himself that he would return to reprise his role as Johnny Silverhand in the Cyberpunk 2077 : Phantom Liberty expansion. [ 1 ] Trivia [ ] During the the 2019 E3 conference, when an audience member shouted out to Keanu "You're breathtaking!", the actor responded back "You're breathtaking! You're all breathtaking!" resulting in a near instantaneous meme. Because of this, Breathtaking became an official achievement in the game, which is unlocked by obtaining all items related to Johnny Silverhand. During the Cyberpunk 2077 Off the Leash quest, Kerry Eurodyne has a similar with a fan.
