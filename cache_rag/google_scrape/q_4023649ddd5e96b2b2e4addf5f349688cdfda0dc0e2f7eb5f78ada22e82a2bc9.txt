Xenoblade Greetings folks, It's been one year since DE was released, and since it came with a remastered soundtrack I figured a commercial release was only a matter of time; the music has been a highlight in every Xenoblade game so far and every previous entry got a well-deserved physical OST. However, as far as I can tell and despite following the Japanese twitter for announcements, there is still nothing in sight. Now it may be the remaster of an existing soundtrack, a first for the series, but I believe it has enough artistic merit to deserve the distinction (if only for the completely new Future Connected tracks), and commercially speaking it seems like a no-brainer to capitalize on. I find it hard to imagine it not happening at all. Did I miss anything about this? Is there usually a pattern with those releases? Read more New to Reddit?
