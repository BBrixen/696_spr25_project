Discussion I've recently been obsessed with the German national team's fall from glory. Looking into it from the talent side of things there seems to be no fault or anything. Germany has incredible talent depth and pretty much excess players for replacement in almost every position. Only thing I can discern is that Hansi Flick is a terrible manager, he has been doing some weird experimentations with players and rotating them in positions where they don't belong at all in recent matches where they suffered loses to "worser" teams. I suspect that this might have to do with the team's morale. Maybe it's the England golden generation situation where they put club over country. And perhaps it's not entirely true that Germany have everything sorted out, as I guess they still have yet to find a replacement for Miroslav Klose.
