Post-Apocalypse [ ] Virginia [ ] Adrienne survived the early stages of the outbreak and eventually found herself as a member of a large group of survivors led by a woman named Natania . Unfortunately, her group would be discovered by a large, hostile organization of survivors led by Simon (who later became Negan 's ruthless and sadistic second-in-command) and under his orders, Adrienne and her people were forced to gather and provide supplies for them under threat of death. Eventually, under Natania's orders, the group initiated a rebellion to fight back against their oppressors. However, due to being outgunned and outnumbered, their group was defeated. As punishment for their defiance and to deter any further resistance, Simon had his group murder every male survivor above the age of 10 in Adrienne's group. It is unknown if any of Adrienne's family were among the casualties of the rebellion or subsequent massacre. Following this and being unwilling to continue working for Simon and his group, Adrienne and her group, consisting mainly of females now, fled their community and established a new home at a location called Oceanside and reestablished their community hidden from them. In order to prevent Simon and his group from finding them again, Natania ordered any survivor who happened upon the community to be immediately killed in order to maintain the secrecy of their new home. Season 7 [ ] " Swear " [ ] Adrienne is alerted about an intruder and searches for Tara in nearby woods. She later helps children with arts and crafts. " Something They Need " [ ] Adrienne is seen chatting with a woman by clothesline, before fleeing from Oceanside after bombs detonate. She later sits with the other captured residents as Rick and Natania make a deal. Afterwards, she returns to the seaside village with everyone else, including the Alexandrians. Season 8 [ ] " Wrath " [ ] Just as the Saviors arrive at the Hilltop to slaughter the people inside, Adrienne, along with Rachel , Cyndie , Kathy , Beatrice , and several Oceanside warriors, ambush the soldiers with Molotov cocktails, effectively wiping them out and saving the town. Season 9 [ ] " The Calm Before " [ ] Adrienne watches from the crowd as Ezekiel declares they've finally accomplished Rick , Carl , and Jesus ' dreams of reuniting the communities. "Let the fair of a new beginning begin!" Ezekiel says as everyone celebrates and applauds. Adrienne then enjoys the fair and socializes with the people of the Coalition. That night, Adrienne attends the theater and watches the movie with other fair attendees. The next day, Adrienne listens from the crowd as Siddiq delivers the tragic news about the fate of the missing fair attendees, tells them how brave the victims were in their final moments and how he was intentionally kept alive to tell this very story. He encourages everyone to remember the fallen as brave heroes and to honor them. Season 10 [ ] " Lines We Cross " [ ] Adrienne walks around the settlement with another woman in the background as Daryl and Michonne talk. Season 11 [ ] " New Haunts " [ ] Adrienne is among the Oceansiders to move to the Commonwealth , and enjoys the Halloween festivities in the city. " Lockdown " [ ] Adrienne attends the protest demanding justice for Sebastian 's crimes. When the soldiers begin clearing the square as a result of the lockdown, she reluctantly leaves along with the other protesters. " A New Deal " [ ] Adrienne attends the Founders' Day festivities in Union Square and flees when zombified city workers attack the crowd. " Variant " [ ] In the aftermath of the undead attack on the Founders' Day festivities, Adrienne attempts to flee Union Square among the dozens of other civilians running and screaming. " What's Been Lost " [ ] Adrienne attends a press conference at Union Station as a news photographer for the Commonwealth Tribune . She is among several Alexandrians and many Commonwealth citizens to applaud after Yumiko reveals she will be defending Eugene Porter in court against Pamela Milton. As an act of retaliation by Pamela, Adrienne is among the last of the Virginia immigrants to be rounded up and deported to Outpost 22. " Faith " [ ] At night, Adrienne is awoken and rounded up with the rest of the imprisoned Alexandrians and Commonwealth exiles and forced outside to the windmill. She is horrified as the prison warden attempts to execute Annie and Negan, but is relived after back-up arrives and the Commonwealth soldiers are forced to surrender, thus liberating everyone. " Family " [ ] Adrienne accompanies the group of Alexandrians and liberated Commonwealth prisoners to Union Station to confront Pamela Milton . She is shot in the leg during an ambush by Commonwealth government agents who open fire on the fighters from the second floor. Despite being injured, Adrienne escapes the train station alive. " Rest in Peace " [ ] With an injured leg, Adrienne flees to the Commonwealth Hospital but is swarmed by the undead. Despite Connie, Kelly, Magna, and Yumiko's best attempts to save Adrienne, she is pulled through the doors by the undead and quickly eaten alive. Death [ ] Killed By Pamela Milton (Caused) Livits (Caused, Accidental) Zombies Walkers break into the hospital and Adrienne is grabbed by them. Despite the best efforts of the group to pull her back in, the walkers overpower them and Adrienne is dragged outside and devoured.
