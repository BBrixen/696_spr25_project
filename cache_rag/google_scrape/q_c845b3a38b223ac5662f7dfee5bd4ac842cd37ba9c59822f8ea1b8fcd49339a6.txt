The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time Re-Arranged Album Games Ocarina of Time Release Date December 22nd, 1999 Credits Publisher Tokuma Japan Communications Composer Koji Kondo Arranged MOKA Attributes Type Soundtrack Length 12 Tracks Catalog Number TKCA-71824 The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time Re-Arranged Album was released on December 22nd, 1999 and is one of three soundtracks released for Ocarina of Time . The album contains 12 tracks, all of which are remixes of songs that appeared within Ocarina of Time .
