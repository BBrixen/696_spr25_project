ET Online Last Updated: Jul 28, 2024, 02:53:00 PM IST Rate Story Follow us Share Font Size Abc Small Abc Medium Abc Large Save Print Comment Synopsis Marvel Studios has announced significant changes to their upcoming Avengers films. Originally titled 'Avengers: The Kang Dynasty,' the film has been rebranded as 'Avengers: Doomsday' due to legal issues surrounding Jonathan Majors, who was set to play Kang the Conqueror. In a surprising move, Robert Downey Jr. will return to the MCU, not as Iron Man, but as the new villain, Doctor Doom. Agencies Robert Downey Jr. Returns as Doctor Doom Marvel Studios has officially announced a major shift in their cinematic universe, replacing the highly anticipated 'Avengers: The Kang Dynasty' with 'Avengers: Doomsday' and introducing Robert Downey Jr. as Doctor Doom. Two years ago, Marvel Studios revealed 'Avengers: The Kang Dynasty' during their Hall H presentation at San Diego Comic-Con. However, due to significant developments over the past two years, the plans for this film have undergone drastic changes. #Pahalgam Terrorist Attack India pulled the plug on IWT when Pakistanis are fighting over water What makes this India-Pakistan standoff more dangerous than past ones The problem of Pakistan couldn't have come at a worse time for D-St Jonathan Majors Controversy and Its Impact Jonathan Majors, who was set to be a central figure in the MCU’s Multiverse Saga as Kang the Conqueror, faced legal issues that led to his conviction for reckless assault in 2023. Following this, Marvel Studios decided to remove Majors from all future projects, necessitating a restructuring of their plans, including the 2026 film 'Avengers: The Kang Dynasty'. The Big Reveal at San Diego Comic-Con 2024 During Marvel Studios' Hall H presentation at San Diego Comic-Con 2024, President Kevin Feige made a surprising announcement. The Russo Brothers, Anthony and Joe Russo, who directed some of Marvel’s most successful films including 'Avengers: Endgame', are returning to direct the next Avengers movies. The upcoming films will be titled 'Avengers: Doomsday' and ' Avengers: Secret Wars '. Feige introduced the Russo Brothers to an enthusiastic audience, and Joe Russo explained their return: "When we directed 'Avengers: Endgame,' we believed it was the end for us in the MCU. However, we found a very special story that convinced us to come back. It’s the biggest story that Marvel comics ever told." Robert Downey Jr. as Doctor Doom The biggest revelation was that Robert Downey Jr. will return to the MCU, not as Iron Man, but as the new central villain, Doctor Doom. This casting choice marks a significant shift in Marvel’s storyline, moving away from Kang and focusing on Doctor Doom as the new antagonist for the Multiverse Saga. The new film, 'Avengers: Doomsday', is set for release in May 2026, followed by 'Avengers: Secret Wars' in May 2027. The Russo Brothers' studio, AGBO, will co-produce these films with Marvel. Screenwriter Stephen McFeely, who has collaborated with the Russos on several Marvel films, will take over the script duties for both 'Doomsday' and 'Secret Wars'. Transition from Kang to Doctor Doom The decision to move away from the Kang storyline was influenced by both the legal issues surrounding Jonathan Majors and the lackluster reception of Kang as a character compared to Thanos from the Infinity Saga. The Season 2 finale of 'Loki' effectively concluded Kang’s storyline, allowing Marvel to pivot without disrupting their broader plans for the Multiverse Saga. The introduction of Doctor Doom, played by Robert Downey Jr., promises a compelling and emotionally charged narrative. The idea of the Avengers facing a villain who looks like Tony Stark, a beloved hero who sacrificed himself in 'Avengers: Endgame', adds a layer of complexity and drama to the upcoming films. Marvel Studios' decision to reframe the next phase of the MCU with 'Avengers: Doomsday' and 'Avengers: Secret Wars' directed by the Russo Brothers, and featuring Robert Downey Jr. as Doctor Doom, marks an exciting new chapter for fans. This shift not only addresses the challenges faced by the franchise but also sets the stage for a fresh and intriguing storyline.
