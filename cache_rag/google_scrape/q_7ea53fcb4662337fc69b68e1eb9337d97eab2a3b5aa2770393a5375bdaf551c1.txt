Brad Penner, USA TODAY Sports Oklahoma QB Baker Mayfield won Walter Camp Award and Davey O'Brien Award, given to college football's player of the year and best QB, respectively. John Amis, AP Oklahoma quarterback Baker Mayfield won the Maxwell Award, another player of the year honor presented by the Maxwell Memorial Football Club of Philadelphia. John Amis, AP Alabama's Minkah Fitzpatrick won the Jim Thorpe Award given to college football's top defensive back. John Amis, AP Alabama safety Minkah Fitzpatrick won the Chuck Bednarik Award, given to the defensive player of the year. John Amis, AP Utah's Matt Gay won Lou Groza Award given to college football's best kicker. John Amis, AP Oklahoma State's James Washington won the Fred Biletnikoff Award,given to college football's best receiver. John Amis, AP Stanford's Bryce Love, won the Doak Walker Award, given to the nation's top running back. John Amis, AP Houston defensive tackle Ed Oliver won the Outland Trophy, given to the nation's best interior lineman. John Amis, AP Texas' Michael Dickson, won the Ray Guy Award, given to the nation's best punter. John Amis, AP Virginia linebacker Micah Kiser won the Campbell Trophy, honoring academic excellence. John Amis, AP Kentucky's Courtney Love received the Wuerffel Trophy, given for community service. Catalina Fragoso, USA TODAY Sports Scott Frost was named The Home Depot Coach of the Year for his work with Central Florida. John Amis, AP Iowa linebacker Josey Jewell won the Lott IMPACT Trophy for outstanding defensive player. Jeffrey Becker, USA TODAY Sports Oklahoma's Mark Andrews won the John Mackey Award, given to the nation's top tight end. Tim Heitman, USA TODAY Sports Georgia's Roquan Smith won the Dick Butkus Award, given to the nation's top linebacker. Dale Zanine, USA TODAY Sports Penn State's Saquon Barkley won the Paul Hornung Award, given to the nation's most versatile player. Rich Barnes, USA TODAY Sports Oklahoma's Mason Rudolph won the Johnny Unitas Golden Arm Award, given to the nation's top senior quarterback. Reese Strickland, USA TODAY Sports Ohio State's Billy Price won the Rimington Trophy, given to the nation's top center. Joseph Maiorana, USA TODAY Sports The "Iowa Wave" to the children of the University of Iowa Children's Hospital received the Disney Spirit Award, given to the most inspirational thing in college football.
