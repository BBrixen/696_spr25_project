Type Live Performance Album The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time Hyrule Symphony is a live performance album released in 1999 in Japan. [ name references needed ] The album has twelve orchestrated versions of songs composed by Koji Kondo from the Ocarina of Time soundtrack , followed by a medley of music from prior games in The Legend of Zelda series . The songs were co-arranged by Ryuichi Katsumata , who also played piano on the album.
