Sal Romano Mar 27 2025 / 9:05 AM EDT 0 Famitsu has published its estimated physical game software data for Japan for the week of March 17, 2025 to March 23, 2025. Xenoblade Chronicles X: Definitive Edition for Switch was the highest selling new release of the week, opening at 74,689 retail copies sold. Atelier Yumia: The Alchemist of Memories & the Envisioned Land came in second, moving 27,810 copies on PlayStation 5 , 26,315 copies on Switch, and 6,949 copies on PlayStation 4 for an opening week total of 61,074 retail units sold. On the hardware side, the Switch family sold 30,504 units, the PlayStation 5 family sold 15,212 units, the Xbox Series family sold 252 units, and the PlayStation 4 family sold 18 units.
