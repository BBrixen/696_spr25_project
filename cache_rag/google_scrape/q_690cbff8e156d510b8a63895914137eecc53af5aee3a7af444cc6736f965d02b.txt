Members Online • Mimebandit102 What most people don't realize when speculating Xenoblade Chronicles: Definitive Edition's release date Discussion When people speculate the release date of Xenoblade Chronicles: Definitive Edition(XC:DE), most will say that it'll release around December due to the fact that the last two games(Xenoblade Chronicles 2 and Xenoblade Chronicles X) were both released in December. There is one major thing that they don't realize, and that is the fact that this year is the 10th anniversary of the original release of Xenoblade Chronicles in Japan on June 10th. And while I think it's possible that XC:DE releases in December, I think it's more likely that it'll release around June 10th, 2020. Read more Archived post.
