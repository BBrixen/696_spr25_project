Tsitsipas was making his tournament debut. It marked the first instance since 2005 , and only the fourth instance overall, that the Tour Finals champion was determined via a final-set tiebreak. [ citation needed ] Alexander Zverev was the defending champion, but was defeated by Thiem in the semifinals. [ 1 ] Alongside Tsitsipas, Daniil Medvedev and Matteo Berrettini made their tournament debuts. This marked the final Tour Finals appearance for six-time champion Roger Federer ; age 38, he lost in the semifinals to Tsitsipas. Rafael Nadal secured the year-end No. 1 ranking for the fifth time after Novak Djokovic was eliminated in the round-robin stage.
