Members Online • Jasasino Splatoon 2 has had plenty of time to develop as a sequel, not just a port I heard of some people questioning the shortness of time to develop Splatoon 2 as a sequel vs a port and so I just wanted show that Splatoon 2 is going to be a full, original game. I was looking back at the Iwata asks session on Splatoon when it first came out, and they were saying that at E3 2014 they had only finished 10% of the game (in their words Link ). Consider that it took 11 months to get it up from 10% to 100% of the launch material. Now Splatoon 2 has likely been in development (at least in the background) since September 2015 as the August update of rainmaker was the last major addition to the Wii U game. That gives them around 1 year and 8 months to develop, or 82% more time (assuming a may release date). Along with the re-use of some materials (e.g. Moray Towers) this should make for quite the packed new Splatoon game. Read more New to Reddit?
