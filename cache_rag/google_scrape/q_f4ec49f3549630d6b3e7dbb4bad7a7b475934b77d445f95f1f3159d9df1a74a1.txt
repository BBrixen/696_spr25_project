This was their fifth appearance . Croatia managed to reach the final where they lost to France and finished second in the tournament. Qualifying [ edit ] See also: 2018 FIFA World Cup qualification – UEFA Group I Croatia was in Group I of UEFA's World Cup qualifications . They played alongside Iceland , Ukraine , Turkey , Finland and Kosovo . Croatia finished as runner-up in the group and entered the second-round play-offs where the team beat Greece and qualified for the 2018 FIFA World Cup . Croatia national team was led by manager Ante Čačić until 7 October 2017 when he was, due to a series of bad results, replaced by Zlatko Dalić .
