The top two candidates, independent Emmanuel Macron and far-right Marine Le Pen, go into a runoff in a fortnight . Find out where each drew most support and what happens next Macron and Le Pen progress – live coverage Seán Clarke and Josh Holder Sun 23 Apr 2017 20.56 EDT First published on Sun 23 Apr 2017 13.15 EDT Share Official first round result With 107 of 107 departements counted | At 17:58 CEST Macron 24.01% Le Pen 21.3% Fillon 20.01% Mélenchon 19.58% Hamon 6.36% Dupont-Aignan 4.7% Lassalle 1.21% Poutou 1.09% Asselineau 0.92% Arthaud 0.64% Cheminade 0.18% The top two candidates go into a run-off election in two weeks' time, on Sunday 7 May. Second-round projection Pollsters Ifop asked voters for the main contenders who they would opt for in the second round, if the remaining candidates were Macron and Le Pen.
