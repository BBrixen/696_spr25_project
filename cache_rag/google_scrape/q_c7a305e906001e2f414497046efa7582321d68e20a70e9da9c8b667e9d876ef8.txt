The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time Re-Arranged Album - Zelda Wiki The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time Re-Arranged Album From Zelda Wiki, the Zelda encyclopedia Jump to navigation Jump to search The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time Re-Arranged Album Game(s) Ocarina of Time Tracks 12 Release Date(s) December 22, 1999 Type Remix Album The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time Re-Arranged Album is one of the official soundtracks for Ocarina of Time . It was released in December 22, 1999 and was arranged by MOKA. Composed of 12 tracks, the album remixes some of the songs from Ocarina of Time .
