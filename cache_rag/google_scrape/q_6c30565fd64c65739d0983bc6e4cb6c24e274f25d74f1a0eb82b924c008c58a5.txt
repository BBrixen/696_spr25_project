Image Forget Shareka, winner of the 2021 Ellie Maxwell Award Life Future Hope Foundation is a non-governmental organisation aiming to transform indigenous and rural farmers’ lives and help eradicate poverty. The charity offers farmers educational opportunities, such as vocational based agribusiness and entrepreneurship training. Forget, an MSC student currently studying Entrepreneurship and Innovation, will receive £500 of funding for her project, as well as mentoring support from Scottish charity, Firefly International. Ellie Maxwell The Ellie Maxwell award aims to encourage and support students who are developing innovative ideas and projects that will promote social change through a charitable project. The award was launched in 2019 at Firefly International’s 20 th anniversary celebrations to continue the legacy of the charity’s founder, Ellie Maxwell. Ellie, who died from cancer ten years ago, launched the charity in 1999 while she was studying at the University of Edinburgh.
