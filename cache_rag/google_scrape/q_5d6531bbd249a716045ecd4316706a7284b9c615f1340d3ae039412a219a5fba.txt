Read more French presidential election of 2017 results Published by Statista Research Department , Aug 6, 2024 This statistic shows the final results of the French Presidential election of 2017, which took place between April 23 and May 7 that year. In the first round, Emmanuel Macron had the highest share of votes with 24.01 percent, followed by Marine Le Pen with 21.3 percent. In the second round of voting, where voters could only choose between Macron and Le Pen, Macron won 66.1 percent of the votes and was elected as French President.
