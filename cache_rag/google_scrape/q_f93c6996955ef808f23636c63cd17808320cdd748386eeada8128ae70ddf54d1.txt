Do I need to redeem both Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers in a set at the same time? General information What are Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers? Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers are a special offer available to Nintendo Switch Online members that can be purchased in a set of two. Each individual voucher can be redeemed for a selected Nintendo Switch digital software title, presenting the opportunity to save on two games. Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers cannot be redeemed for games exclusive to the Nintendo Switch 2 system. Users with an active, paid Nintendo Switch Online membership are eligible to purchase and redeem vouchers. If your Nintendo Switch Online membership ends before you redeem a voucher, you will still be able to use the voucher as long as you begin a new membership before the voucher expires. In Quebec, Canada, this offer is only available to users 13 and older. What does a set of Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers cost? Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers are sold in sets of two vouchers. Any applicable sales tax is added at the time the pair of vouchers is purchased, not when redeeming individual vouchers. How to check the price of a Nintendo Switch Game Voucher set To find the price of a set of Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers in your country, select the appropriate option: Argentina: https://www.nintendo.com/es-ar/store/products/nintendo-switch-game-vouchers/ Brazil: https://www.nintendo.com/pt-br/store/products/nintendo-switch-game-vouchers/ Canada (English): https://www.nintendo.com/en-ca/store/products/nintendo-switch-game-vouchers/ Canada (French): https://www.nintendo.com/fr-ca/store/products/nintendo-switch-game-vouchers/ Chile: https://www.nintendo.com/es-cl/store/products/nintendo-switch-game-vouchers/ Colombia: https://www.nintendo.com/es-co/store/products/nintendo-switch-game-vouchers/ Mexico: https://www.nintendo.com/es-mx/store/products/nintendo-switch-game-vouchers/ Peru: https://www.nintendo.com/es-pe/store/products/nintendo-switch-game-vouchers/ United States: https://www.nintendo.com/store/products/nintendo-switch-game-vouchers/ Which countries are eligible for this offer? Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers are available to purchase in Argentina, Brazil, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Mexico, Peru, and the United States. Vouchers can only be redeemed for titles in the same region that the vouchers were purchased in. Is there a limit to the number of Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers I can purchase? Yes. You cannot have more than eight active vouchers at a time. If you currently have eight active vouchers, after using two vouchers you can purchase another set. When do Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers expire? Unused vouchers expire one year (365 days) after the date of purchase. Please redeem your vouchers before they expire. How can I check how many Game Vouchers I have remaining, and what their expiration dates are? You can see your available, unused Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers in the Nintendo Switch Online section of your Nintendo Account Shop Menu, where each individual voucher will be listed along with its expiration date. How to check voucher status on Nintendo Switch Your vouchers and their expiration dates can also be found using your Nintendo Switch: Launch the Nintendo eShop from the HOME Menu using the Nintendo Account that purchased the vouchers. Select your User icon in the top-right corner. Select Nintendo Switch Online in the left menu. Scroll down to the Nintendo Switch Game Voucher section. Each individual voucher will be listed along with its expiry date. How can I check my Nintendo Switch Game Voucher purchase and redemption history? You can see the voucher sets you have purchased and how your vouchers have been redeemed from the Purchase History menu on accounts.nintendo.com. How to check your eShop Purchase History Online Visit the Nintendo Account website , and sign in to your Nintendo Account. From the profile settings, select Shop menu . Select Purchase History . Here, you can view account activity dating back up to two years. This includes any promotional or bundled download codes that came with your purchases. For more details, please read How to View Nintendo Switch eShop Purchase History Are Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers purchased before March 24, 2025 eligible for My Nintendo Gold Points? Yes. If you purchased a set of Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers on or before 9:30 PM, March 24, 2025, you received Gold Points for the purchase. You will not receive additional Gold Points when you redeem a voucher on a game. Game voucher redemptions may be eligible for My Nintendo promotions. Review the promotion details for more information. Can I get a refund for a Nintendo Switch Game Voucher? Except as authorized by Nintendo or as required by applicable law, vouchers cannot be refunded or exchanged for any other item, including after they have expired. Purchasing Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers How do I purchase Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers? Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers can be purchased in sets of two through Nintendo eShop for Nintendo Switch or through the Nintendo website. For more information, please read How to Purchase Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers . Can I gift Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers to a friend? No. Vouchers can only be redeemed using the same Nintendo Account that purchased them. Redeeming Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers Do I need a Nintendo Switch Online membership to redeem a Nintendo Switch Game Voucher? Yes. You must be part of an active, paid membership to redeem a voucher. If your membership ends while you have an unused voucher, you can still use the voucher after you begin any new paid Nintendo Switch Online membership before the voucher expires. The voucher expiration date will not be extended for the time you do not have an active membership. Any game redeemed with a voucher will be available to the Nintendo Account that redeemed it regardless of Nintendo Switch Online membership status. How do I redeem a Nintendo Switch Game Voucher? A voucher can be redeemed in the Nintendo eShop on Nintendo Switch or through the Nintendo website. For detailed steps, please read How to Redeem a Nintendo Switch Game Voucher . Which games are eligible to be redeemed with a Nintendo Switch Game Voucher? You can view the list of current and future games available to be redeemed with a voucher either in the Nintendo eShop using your Nintendo Switch, or by visiting the appropriate page on the Nintendo website. Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers cannot be redeemed for games exclusive to the Nintendo Switch 2 system. How to check eligible titles on Nintendo Switch Launch the Nintendo eShop from the HOME Menu. Select Nintendo Switch Online in the left menu. Scroll down and select Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers . Select View Eligible Software to view the list of current and future eligible games. A future eligible title can be redeemed with a voucher after it is made available for pre-order. For more information about pre-orders and Nintendo Switch Game Vouchers, please read the Nintendo eShop Pre-Order and Pre-Load FAQ . Will there be more games added to the list of eligible titles that can be redeemed with a Nintendo Switch Game Voucher? Yes. As new eligible games are released or made available for pre-order, they will be added to the list of games available to be redeemed with a voucher. Games that will be eligible to redeem with a voucher at a later date will also be presented in this list.
